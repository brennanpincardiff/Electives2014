About the survey and these pages

We would like to thank the seventy five (75) students who responded to the Elective survey.

These pages were prepared by:

  • Dr Paul Brennan works in the Institute of Cancer & Genetics, School of Medicine, Cardiff University.
  • Dr Sam Hibbitts is the SSC Director in MB BCh at Cardiff University.

These pages were prepared using:

  • D3.js - an open source javascript library for data visualisation.
  • Code and advice from Mike Bostock, techslides.com, D3.js Tips and Tricks and others not mentioned.
  • Bootstrap - a HTML, CSS, and JS framework.
  • The topic clouds were prepared using Text is Beautiful.
  • Getting d3 tool tips to work within Bootstrap required the use of d3.tip by Justin Palmer - thanks Justin.
