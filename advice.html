Advice from Cardiff University Students about Electives

Start planning early. Focus on an aspect of medicine that you enjoy. Make the most of the experience and enjoy the time you have. Prepare in advance by doing a basic language course ahead of the Elective.

"Make sure you enjoy the elective and not just the travelling"

"Bring alcohol gel! Prepare yourself mentally for a completely different attitude"

"Go outside your comfort zone and make the most of it!!"
